ORDER CANCELLATION POLICY

Order cancellation rules and procedures:

1. Orders with status PLACED can be cancelled at any time before shipping.
2. Orders with status SHIPPED can be cancelled, but may incur shipping fees.
3. Orders with status DELIVERED cannot be cancelled.
4. Orders with status CANCELLED are already cancelled and require no further action.

Cancellation process:
- Customer must request cancellation through support or account portal.
- Cancellation requests are reviewed within 24 hours.
- If order has already shipped, customer may need to return the item.
- Refunds for cancelled orders are processed automatically.

Special circumstances:
- Delayed orders (past expected delivery date) may be eligible for cancellation with full refund.
- Orders that are significantly delayed (more than 7 days past expected date) are automatically eligible for cancellation.
