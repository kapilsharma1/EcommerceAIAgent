REFUND POLICY

Customers may request refunds for orders that meet the following criteria:

1. Orders must be cancelled within 24 hours of placement for a full refund.
2. Orders that have not yet shipped are eligible for full refunds.
3. Shipped orders may be eligible for partial refunds if returned within 30 days of delivery.
4. Delivered orders can be refunded if the product is defective or not as described.
5. Digital products are non-refundable unless defective.
6. Custom or personalized items are non-refundable.
7. Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

For order cancellations:
- Orders can be cancelled if status is PLACED or SHIPPED.
- Cancelled orders are automatically refunded if payment was already processed.
- Once an order is DELIVERED, it cannot be cancelled but may be eligible for return/refund.
